## 1. Executive Summary

**Main Goal:**  
To establish a Tier-3 private AI infrastructure that allows institutions to host, train, and deploy large-scale LLMs locally, ensuring 100% data sovereignty and zero recurring cloud costs.

**Problem:**  
Cloud AI (OpenAI/Google) is expensive at scale (₹3.5L+/month for 10k users), lacks privacy for institutional data, and suffers from internet latency.

**Solution:**  
A high-performance "Ultimate AI Node" featuring **4x NVIDIA RTX 5090 (128GB VRAM)**. This local powerhouse serves up to **1,50,000 users** with sub-second response times using fine-tuned "Master-Worker" swarms.

**Financial Highlight:**  
- **Total Capex:** ₹35,00,000 (One-time investment).
- **Monthly Net Profit:** ₹23,50,000 (at 1 Lakh users).
- **Payback Period:** < 2 Months post-scale.
